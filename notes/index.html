Goals as a club

The purpose of the Programming Club is to learn how to code! No experience is required. It's a great way to collaborate with people who share your same interests. We reach out to many other organization and clubs to provide them with state-of-the-art technology such as websites, apps, and games. It's a great way to meet new people and code!

Who should join?

Everyone is welcome to join programming club! We have a wide variety of students with many different backgrounds in programming. This is the perfect place to learn how to code, perfect your skills, or practice teaching programming to others
